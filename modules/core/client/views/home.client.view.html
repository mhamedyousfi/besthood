ABOUT US & Best Hood

Best Hood is a platform for locals to share all things they would like to change in their neighbourhood.Our platform aims to bring closer the authorities and citizens in order to improve their situation. We're a team of designers and coders working to create beautiful, human-friendly digital experiences for important causes. We passionately believe that design & technology can be a powerful force for change.
